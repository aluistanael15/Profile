The Early Years

History

In April 2013, two long-time friends named Luis Tanael and Ken Roth enjoyed their summer together by riding their bicycles in Clark Air Base. In November 2014, LMSI Boys created a Soccer Team named Angeles Soccer Team (AST) it was dissolved in November 2015 and decided to create a multi sports group in Angeles City. By December 26, 2015, the first ride of the founders named Ken Roth, Kevin Robson, and Luis Tanael at Sacobia Bridge, Clark Pampanga and formed the group Angeles Bikers Club in that same day. Angeles Bikers Club (ABC) opened its doors to a modest cyclist in Angeles City. Its primary purpose was to offer and promote Physical Fitness exercise, friendship, adventures of unseen places, food trippin, and many more. It was organized as a non-profit organization oriented towards to serve solo bikers and the community.

Founders

The founders appointed the leadership of Ralf Vincent Dela Pena as the leader of 2017, commitment and perseverance brought increasingly members around Angeles City and San Fernando. In that same year in a half term John Adrian Sanchez was appointed as a leader of ABC, now a leader of the Alpha Cycling Team (ACT) a road biker cyclist was formed in 2018. Consequently, the Angeles Bikers Club (ABC) became the first group in Angeles City to create a montage videos for the purpose of memories in the future. Angeles Bikers Club created the rules of the group that members vote their Leader to lead the group for one-year term. On 2018, Luis Tanael was the 4th leader of ABC an Information Technology as Network Administrator persistent and determination to improve the group by creating more montage videos and promote Angeles Bikers Club in the community. In 2019, solo bikers progressively on joining Angeles Bikers Club. In February 2020, Jun Ocampo was elected as a 6th leader of ABC, an Information Technology as Web Developer at Clark Development Corporation and President of Chess Club of Angeles City.

Gift Giving

In April 2017, the ABC Foundation was formed to help the “Kapos Palad” families in Angeles City. Since then ABC Foundation is organizing every year to help street children and homeless people to make them smile in the month of December “Giving Goods to the Poor”. By 2018, ABC Foundation was dissolved and created a Charity named “Gift Giving”.
