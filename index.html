Welcome to Angeles Bikers Club

Mission

To help solo bikers around Angeles City to encourage them to travel seek adventure and make a lot of new friends by meeting different people in different places. We make our bikers to meet or even exceed their expectations and abilities when it comes to biking.

Vision

To be the center of the Bicycle community not only in Angeles City but the whole Province of Pampanga, through cyclist satisfaction, social responsibilities, and doing community services. Also, a safe choice for people of all cultures, ages, abilities, and backgrounds.
